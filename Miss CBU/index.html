Star Power: Miss CBU Nomsa Bwalya Phiri Takes Flight

Move over textbooks, CBU's got a new brainiac with a crown who walks the halls with more swagger than a supermodel on a Milan runway! Say hello to Miss CBU herself, Nomsa Bwalya Phiri. This beauty with brains juggles studies, environmental advocacy, and the occasional existential crisis (we've all been there, right?) with the grace of a seasoned pro strutting the catwalk of life.

Childhood Echoes:

Nomsa's story begins not under the spotlight, but amidst the emerald embrace of Luanshya. Unlike a contestant confined to the sterile backstage of a competition, her childhood unfolded as an adventure in the vibrant "woods" (think lush thickets!).

A young Nomsa, armed with boundless curiosity and a loyal crew of friends, conquering these wild spaces, collecting nature's candy (wild fruits!), and forging an unbreakable bond with her close-knit community. "Everyone's child was everyone's responsibility," Nomsa recalls fondly, a testament to the spirit of togetherness that nurtured her.

Their symphony of laughter echoed through the trees as they played "Bonga," a local game that brought the neighborhood together like a magnet attracting cool kids. It all culminated in a legendary Bonga tournament – a memory that still sparks joy and a sense of community in Nomsa's eyes. These early experiences, both wild and heartwarming, undoubtedly shaped the passionate and responsible leader she is today.

Favorite Teachers & A Literary Spark

"Picking favorites is tough," Nomsa admits with a warm smile, "but Mr. Kunda (biology) and Mrs. Phiri (literature) definitely sparked something special." Turns out, Mrs. Phiri's influence ignited a lifelong love for books, with Ngozi Adichie's “The Concubine” a powerful novel she had read in high school,becoming a treasured read.

Beyond Beauty: A Boxer in the ring

Nomsa is more than just a pretty face and a crown. She's an advocate with a mission, fiercely dedicated to protecting the environment and championing the rights of children. This passion for change stems from her deep connection with nature, forged during her childhood adventures in Luanshya. Don't worry, though, her love for the environment extends beyond creepy-crawlies – snakes still send shivers down her spine! We all have our kryptonite, right? Even superheroes (and Miss CBU is a superhero in her own right) get the occasional scare.

THE PLACE I TOOK AFTER

CBU isn't just a place of learning for Nomsa; it's a "cognitive bootcamp," pushing her intellectual boundaries further than a student trying to explain the economic theory of comparative advantage to their pet goldfish. Weekends, however, are a different story. While textbooks might still make an appearance (because let's be real, catching up never truly ends), Nomsa also uses this time to unwind and explore her interests. Think of it as a runway model taking a well-deserved break between fittings – essential for maintaining peak performance, both on the catwalk and in the classroom.

The Miss CBU Sash: A Platform for Amplification

The Miss CBU sash isn't just an accessory; it's a platform for Nomsa to amplify her voice and connect with a wider audience. "It gives me a voice and a chance to connect," Nomsa beams. "Plus, networking is my superpower" Her smile widens as she emphasizes, "Being Miss CBU allows me to advocate for children and the environment, and that's a win-win."
